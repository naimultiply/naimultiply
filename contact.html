Board Membership Opportunity

We offer a unique opportunity for our most generous supporters to play a direct role in shaping the future of our organization.

The first 10 donors who contribute over €25,000 will earn a management position on our Board of Directors. This exclusive opportunity allows our most committed supporters to have a direct voice in leading our organization's future direction.

Board members will participate in strategic decision-making, help set organizational priorities, and guide our expansion efforts. This is a chance to be part of building a groundbreaking trust management organization from the ground up.

Ownership Structure:

The overall managing trust will own 10%. Each board member position will own 1% of the organization. The remaining 80% will be owned by all other members of the organization collectively.

For more information about this opportunity, please contact us directly at nai.multiply@gmail.com with "Board Membership Inquiry" in the subject line.
